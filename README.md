# Promotional Materials Portal

Professional file delivery for teams, partners, and clients who need a smoother way to share promotional materials.

The Promotional Materials Portal brings everything into one secure, organized space. It helps teams deliver files with confidence, keeps client materials easy to find, and replaces scattered chats and email attachments with a cleaner, more reliable experience.

## Why It Matters

When promotional files are shared through too many places, things quickly become messy. Important materials get buried, the wrong version gets sent, and it becomes harder for teams and clients to stay aligned.

This system is designed to solve that. It gives everyone a clear place to work, a clear process to follow, and a better overall experience from request to delivery.

## What The System Does

- Keeps promotional files neatly organized in one place
- Lets the right people upload, view, and download materials
- Gives each client a dedicated space for their files
- Allows clients to request new materials when needed
- Helps teams stay informed about file activity and updates
- Makes it possible to recover files removed by mistake

## Who It Is For

This system is built for four types of users:

- Admins who oversee access, assignments, and deadlines
- Production staff who prepare, manage, and upload client materials
- Agents who view and download approved files
- Clients who request materials and access the files prepared for them

## Premium Experience, Practical Benefits

- A more polished way to deliver files to clients and partners
- Less confusion about roles, access, and responsibility
- Faster access to the right materials when they are needed
- A cleaner, more professional client experience
- Better organization without the back-and-forth of scattered messages

## How It Works

1. A client account is created.
2. The client sends a request for materials.
3. The system prepares the client's file space when needed.
4. Production staff upload the needed files.
5. The client and approved users can access and download the finished materials.

## Access And Privacy

Each person only sees what they are allowed to see. Clients can only access their own files, helping keep shared materials private, secure, and well organized.

## Built For Better Delivery

Whether the goal is to send campaign files, provide client-ready materials, or keep internal delivery more organized, the portal is built to make the process feel smoother, safer, and more professional from start to finish.
